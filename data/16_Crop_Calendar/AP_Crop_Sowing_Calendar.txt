ANDHRA PRADESH - CROP SOWING CALENDAR (Quick Reference)
======================================================

SEASON CLASSIFICATION:
---------------------
- KHARIF (Monsoon): June-November
- RABI (Winter): November-March  
- SUMMER: February-June

MAJOR CROPS - SOWING & HARVESTING SCHEDULE:
==========================================

CEREALS:
--------
PADDY:
  Kharif: Sow June-July → Harvest Nov-Dec (135-150 days)
  Rabi: Sow Nov-Dec → Harvest March-April (120-135 days)
  Summer: Sow Jan-Feb → Harvest May-June (110-120 days)

MAIZE:
  Kharif: Sow June-July → Harvest Oct-Nov (90-110 days)
  Rabi: Sow Oct-Nov → Harvest Feb-March (90-100 days)

JOWAR:
  Kharif: Sow June-July → Harvest Oct-Nov (110-120 days)
  Rabi: Sow Sept-Oct → Harvest Jan-Feb (100-110 days)

PULSES:
-------
TUR/ARHAR (Red Gram):
  Kharif: Sow June-July → Harvest Dec-Feb (150-180 days)

GREENGRAM:
  Kharif: Sow June-July → Harvest Sept-Oct (60-75 days)
  Summer: Sow Feb-March → Harvest April-May (60-70 days)

BLACKGRAM:
  Kharif: Sow June-July → Harvest Sept-Oct (70-90 days)
  Rabi: Sow Oct-Nov → Harvest Jan-Feb (80-90 days)

BENGAL GRAM (Chana):
  Rabi: Sow Oct-Nov → Harvest Feb-March (100-120 days)

OILSEEDS:
---------
GROUNDNUT:
  Kharif: Sow June-July → Harvest Oct-Nov (105-120 days)
  Rabi: Sow Oct-Nov → Harvest Jan-Feb (100-110 days)
  Summer: Sow Jan-Feb → Harvest April-May (95-105 days)

SUNFLOWER:
  Kharif: Sow June-July → Harvest Sept-Oct (85-95 days)
  Rabi: Sow Oct-Nov → Harvest Jan-Feb (90-100 days)

CASTOR:
  Kharif: Sow June-July → Harvest Oct-Dec (150-180 days, 3-4 pickings)

CASH CROPS:
-----------
COTTON:
  Kharif: Sow June-July → Pickings Oct-April (150-180 days)
  First Picking: 140-150 DAS
  Last Picking: 200-210 DAS (4-6 pickings total)

SUGARCANE:
  Adsali: Plant July-Aug → Harvest Dec-Feb next year (15-18 months)
  Pre-seasonal: Plant Oct-Nov → Harvest Dec-March (12-14 months)
  Seasonal: Plant Jan-March → Harvest Dec-March next year (12 months)

CHILLI:
  Kharif: Sow May-June → Harvest Oct-Feb (140-150 days)
  Rabi: Sow Aug-Sept → Harvest Jan-April (130-140 days)

TURMERIC:
  Planting: May-June → Harvesting: Feb-March (8-9 months)

VEGETABLES (Season-wise):
========================

KHARIF VEGETABLES (June-July sowing):
- Okra, Brinjal, Chilli, Tomato
- Ridge Gourd, Bitter Gourd, Bottle Gourd, Pumpkin

RABI VEGETABLES (Sept-Nov sowing):
- Tomato, Cabbage, Cauliflower
- Onion, Carrot, Radish, Beans, Peas

SUMMER VEGETABLES (Jan-Feb sowing):
- Cucumber, Watermelon, Muskmelon, Ash Gourd

DISTRICT-WISE CROPPING PATTERN:
===============================

COASTAL ANDHRA (Krishna, Godavari):
  Kharif: Paddy, Maize, Pulses
  Rabi: Paddy, Pulses
  Summer: Paddy, Groundnut, Watermelon
  Perennial: Sugarcane, Coconut, Banana

RAYALASEEMA (Anantapur, Kurnool, Kadapa):
  Kharif: Groundnut, Cotton, Jowar, Bajra, Redgram
  Rabi: Groundnut, Bengal Gram, Sunflower
  Summer: Groundnut, Watermelon
  Perennial: Mango

NORTH COASTAL (Visakhapatnam, Vizianagaram):
  Kharif: Paddy, Maize, Ragi, Pulses
  Rabi: Paddy, Pulses
  Perennial: Cashew, Mango

INTERCROPPING SYSTEMS:
=====================
- Cotton + Red gram (2:1 or 3:1)
- Cotton + Greengram (2:2)
- Cotton + Groundnut (2:2)
- Groundnut + Red gram (4:2)
- Castor + Greengram (1:2)

CROP ROTATION (Irrigated):
==========================
Pattern 1: Paddy → Paddy → Greengram
Pattern 2: Paddy → Blackgram → Paddy
Pattern 3: Kharif Groundnut → Rabi Bengal Gram → Summer Greengram

CRITICAL REMINDERS:
==================
✓ Sowing dates depend on monsoon onset - adjust accordingly
✓ Use certified seeds from authorized dealers
✓ Register crop in E-Panta (mandatory for schemes)
✓ Get crop insured within cutoff dates
✓ Follow recommended varieties for your region
✓ Soil testing every 2-3 years

WEATHER-BASED ADJUSTMENTS:
=========================
- MONSOON DELAY: Use short-duration varieties, delay sowing
- EXCESS RAINFALL: Ensure proper drainage
- MID-SEASON DROUGHT: Life-saving irrigation at critical stages
- CYCLONE ALERT: Harvest mature crops before cyclone
