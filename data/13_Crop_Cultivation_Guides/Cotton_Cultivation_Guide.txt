COTTON CULTIVATION GUIDE - ANDHRA PRADESH
=========================================

NOTE: For official research publications, contact:
- Central Institute for Cotton Research (CICR), Nagpur
- Regional Agricultural Research Station (RARS), AP
- Department of Agriculture, AP

BASIC INFORMATION:
-----------------
- Season: Kharif (June-April) - 150-180 days
- Major Districts: Kurnool, Anantapur, Kadapa, Prakasam
- Average Yield: 18-22 quintals/ha (rainfed), 24-28 quintals/ha (irrigated)

POPULAR BT COTTON VARIETIES:
----------------------------
Short Duration (150-160 days): RCH 2 Bt, Bunny Bt, JK Ishwar
Medium Duration (160-170 days): RCH 650 BGII, Mallika Bt
Long Duration (170-180 days): RCH 791 (ELS), Ankur 3028

CULTIVATION STEPS:
-----------------
1. LAND PREPARATION: Deep summer ploughing (20-25 cm)
2. SEED TREATMENT: Fungicide + Insecticide + Bio-fertilizer
3. SOWING: Dibbling, 2-3 seeds per hill
4. SPACING: 90cm x 60cm (rainfed), 120cm x 60cm (irrigated)
5. THINNING: 15-20 days, retain 1-2 plants/hill

FERTILIZER MANAGEMENT (Bt Cotton - Irrigated):
--------------------------------------------
NPK: 150:75:75 kg/ha
Basal: 25% N + Full P&K + FYM 5-10 tonnes/ha
Top Dressing: 
  - 1st (30-35 DAS): 25% N
  - 2nd (60-65 DAS): 25% N
  - 3rd (90 DAS): 25% N

MAJOR PESTS & MANAGEMENT:
------------------------
1. AMERICAN BOLLWORM: 
   - Pheromone traps, NPV, Emamectin benzoate
   - ETL: 8-10% damaged squares/bolls

2. PINK BOLLWORM:
   - CRITICAL: Destroy crop residues after harvest
   - Pheromone traps (15/ha), Thiodicarb
   - ETL: 10% rosette flowers

3. WHITEFLY:
   - Yellow sticky traps, Spiromesifen
   - Avoid excess nitrogen
   - ETL: 5-10 adults/leaf

4. JASSIDS:
   - Thiamethoxam, Acetamiprid
   - ETL: 1-2 nymphs/leaf

INTEGRATED PEST MANAGEMENT (IPM):
---------------------------------
- Pheromone traps: 10-15/ha
- Light traps: 1/ha
- Border crops: Marigold (trap crop)
- Refuge planting: 20% non-Bt cotton (IRM strategy)
- Biological control: Chrysoperla, Trichogramma

MAJOR DISEASES:
--------------
- Wilt: Crop rotation, resistant varieties, Trichoderma
- Bacterial Blight: Streptocycline + Copper oxychloride
- Grey Mildew: Sulfur dust

IRRIGATION:
----------
Critical Stages: 
  - Square formation (40-45 DAS)
  - Flowering (65-70 DAS)
  - Boll development (90-95 DAS)

Drip Irrigation: Recommended, 40-50% water saving
  - Subsidy: 55% for small/marginal farmers under PMKSY

PICKING:
-------
- Number of pickings: 4-6 (at 15-20 day intervals)
- Time: Morning hours (avoid moisture)
- First picking: 140-150 DAS
- Last picking: 200-210 DAS

ECONOMIC ANALYSIS (Rainfed Bt Cotton):
-------------------------------------
Cost of Cultivation: Rs. 45,000-50,000/ha
Expected Yield: 15 quintals/ha
MSP (2024): Rs. 7,121/quintal (Medium Staple)
Gross Income: Rs. 1,06,815/ha
Net Profit: Rs. 55,000-65,000/ha

MARKETING:
---------
- Cotton Corporation of India (CCI): MSP procurement
- Private traders: Open market
- E-Panta registration: Mandatory for MSP
- Grading: Grade I (clean, white) gets best price

IMPORTANT REMINDERS:
-------------------
✓ Use only certified Bt cotton seeds (check trait authenticity)
✓ Destroy crop residues immediately after harvest (pink bollworm management)
✓ Maintain refuge area (20% non-Bt) for IRM
✓ Avoid monocropping - rotate with non-cotton crops
✓ Register crop in E-Panta for MSP procurement
✓ Get crop insured under PMFBY
