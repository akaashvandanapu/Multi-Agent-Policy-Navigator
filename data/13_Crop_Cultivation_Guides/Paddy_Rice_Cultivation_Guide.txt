PADDY (RICE) CULTIVATION GUIDE - ANDHRA PRADESH
================================================

NOTE: This is a compiled reference guide. For official research publications, contact:
- ANGRAU (Acharya N.G. Ranga Agricultural University)
- ICAR Research Stations
- Department of Agriculture, AP

BASIC INFORMATION:
-----------------
- Seasons: Kharif (June-Nov), Rabi (Nov-Mar), Summer (Feb-Jun)
- Major Districts: Krishna, Godavari, Guntur
- Average Yield: 5,500-7,000 kg/ha

POPULAR VARIETIES:
-----------------
Kharif: BPT 5204 (Samba Mashuri), MTU 1010, MTU 7029 (Swarna)
Rabi: MTU 1010, RNR 15048
Hybrids: DRRH 3, DRRH 4

CULTIVATION STEPS:
-----------------
1. NURSERY: 25-30 days, seed rate 20-25 kg/ha
2. LAND PREPARATION: Puddling, leveling, bund formation
3. TRANSPLANTING: Spacing 20x15 cm, 2-3 seedlings/hill
4. FERTILIZERS: 120:60:40 NPK kg/ha (split application)
5. IRRIGATION: Maintain 5-7 cm water during critical stages
6. HARVESTING: When 80% grains turn golden yellow

MAJOR PESTS:
-----------
- Stem Borer: Use pheromone traps, Chlorantraniliprole
- Brown Plant Hopper: Avoid excess nitrogen, use Buprofezin
- Leaf Folder: Acephate or Cartap hydrochloride

MAJOR DISEASES:
--------------
- Blast: Use resistant varieties (NLR 34449), Tricyclazole
- Sheath Blight: Hexaconazole, good drainage
- Bacterial Leaf Blight: Copper oxychloride + Streptocycline

FERTILIZER SCHEDULE:
-------------------
Basal: 50% N + Full P&K + FYM 10 tonnes/ha
1st Top Dressing (25 DAT): 25% N
2nd Top Dressing (50 DAT): 25% N

ECONOMIC ANALYSIS:
-----------------
Cost of Cultivation: Rs. 40,000-45,000/ha
Expected Yield: 5.5 tonnes/ha
MSP (2024): Rs. 2,300/quintal (Common), Rs. 2,320/quintal (Grade A)
Gross Income: Rs. 1,26,500-1,27,600/ha
Net Profit: Rs. 80,000-85,000/ha
