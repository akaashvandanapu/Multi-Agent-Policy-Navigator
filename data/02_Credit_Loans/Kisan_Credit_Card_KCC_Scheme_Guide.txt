KISAN CREDIT CARD (KCC) SCHEME - ANDHRA PRADESH
===============================================

NOTE: This is a compiled guide. For official documents, please download from:
- Ministry of Agriculture: https://agricoop.gov.in
- NABARD: https://www.nabard.org

OVERVIEW:
---------
The Kisan Credit Card scheme provides credit support to farmers for cultivation and other agricultural needs. Implemented through banks in Andhra Pradesh.

KEY FEATURES:
------------
- Interest Rate: 7% per annum (up to Rs. 3 lakhs)
- Interest Subvention: Additional 3% for prompt repayment
- Effective Rate: As low as 4% for timely payment
- Loan Amount: Based on land holding and crop pattern
- Validity: 5 years (subject to annual review)

ELIGIBILITY:
-----------
- All farmers (individual/joint borrowers)
- Owner cultivators
- Tenant farmers and sharecroppers
- SHGs/JLGs of farmers
- Age: 18-75 years

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
------------------
1. Application form
2. Land ownership proof (Pattadar Passbook)
3. Identity Proof (Aadhaar/Voter ID)
4. Address Proof
5. Passport size photographs

APPLICATION PROCESS:
-------------------
1. Visit nearest bank branch or RSK (Rythu Seva Kendram)
2. Submit application with documents
3. Bank assesses credit requirement
4. KCC issued within 7-15 working days
